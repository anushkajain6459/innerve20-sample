Get ready for the biggest technical fest of IGDTUW

THE ANNUAL TECH FEST OF IGDTUW

Innerve relies on many things;Human resource,public relations,the audience and many more.Yet,to push such a grand fest together you need to glue all these scattered peices.The hardwork puts you where good luck can find you.. With its inception in 2015, every year, the two-day extravaganza has witnessed a phenomenol gathering of students from all domains. The event is back for its FIFTH edition where it aims to be a platform for participants to test their engineering skills and showcase their prowess.
